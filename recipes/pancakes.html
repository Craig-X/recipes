Pancakes

Makes appoximately 6 sweet American-style pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 400g milk
  • 300g plain flour
  • 60g sugar
  • 10g baking powder
  • pinch baking soda
  • 4g salt
  • 10-20g melted butter

Method

  1. Beat the egg, and then add in the milk, whisking until combined
  2. Add the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Whisk until combined, taking care not to overmix. Remember that it's better to have a few small lumps than overmixed batter.
  3. Melt a small knob (Approx 10-20g) butter and stream into the mixture whilst mixing. Then allow the mixture to rest for a few minutes while you heat your pan.
  4. Cook the pancakes in a teflon pan on a medium heat, using a ladle to spoon into the pan. Cook each side for 2-3 minutes or until golden brown.

Make sure that you serve the pancakes hot, and add the toppings of your choice. Nutella works really well, although maple syrup with a knob of butter is very hard to beat.

Enjoy!
